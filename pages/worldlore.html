Valor

Pyromancy Militaristic Empire Seraphine(Firebringer)

Description

Valor is a kingdom forged in fire, both literal and metaphorical. Its soldiers are the steel edge of imperial ambition, and its nobility guard the right to channel magic through sanctioned guilds. Valor is fiercely proud, but beneath its banners of flame lies deep unrest: the disappearance of Princess Elira Flamereign, heir to the throne, has stoked human-elf tensions into near war. Rumors whisper she was stolen by Aleris half-elves, others that she willingly fled into shadow cults.

Ruler

Emperor Aldric Flamereign III

Magic Discipline

Pyromancy & Martial Invocation

Capital

Pyrehold

Exports

Steel, Warhorses, Grain, Mercenaries

Hidden Wounds

  • The Mad King's Echo: A knight of Valor who birthed the Cult of the Embered Hand 300 years ago. They whisper their "true king" lives still in the Cycle, waiting to greet them in death.

  • Blightwardens: Once proud, the Blightwardens fractured - the righteous burn plague with ash and fire, but a twisted sect seeks to control Shadowbeast as living weapons.
