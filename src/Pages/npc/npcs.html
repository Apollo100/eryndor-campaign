Aldric Flamereign

The Iron Regent
Valor Lawful Neutral Leader
The rightful Emperor of the human Empire of Valor.

Description

Tall and broad shouldered, who has the physique of a career soldier who has not let the comforts of rulership make him soft. His hair, once likely sun-bleached, is now a severe, short-cropped steel grey, a legacy of stress and responsibility. His most striking feature is his eyes: the cold, piercing grey of a winter sky, capable of silencing a war council with a single glance. He is never seen without the Crown of Cinders, a simple and brutal circlet of blackened iron shaped like a lion's mane, with two small, ever burning flames flickering where the beast eyes should be. He favors functional, imposing plate armor, and his greatsword "Oathkeeper" is always at his side.

Hroger Blackiron

Dwarven Ruler of Draemir
Draemir Lawful Neutral Leader
Bear-Lord of Draemir

Description

Unlike any dwarf before him, Hrogar stands nearly the height of a man, his broad frame a strange echo of human stature. His jet-black hair falls long and unbound, and his chin is bare — an unthinkable sight in dwarven culture. This has sparked whispers that he is “stone-marked,” shaped differently by Thorne for a purpose. He carries the rune-etched Maul of the Stonefather, its head cracked but still mighty. His presence is commanding yet unsettling, embodying both dwarven resilience and otherness.

Demihuman Clan Leader
Norveth Chaotic Good Leader
The Steel-Jawed Liberator

Description

A towering lionborn demihuman, nearly seven feet tall, with a mane like a stormcloud and golden eyes that burn with fury. His lower jaw is a grotesque-yet-powerful forged plate of steel, forced upon him during his days as a pit fighter slave. Instead of breaking him, it made him a legend. His roar now echoes like a warhorn of metal and thunder. His body is crisscrossed with scars, each one a mark of survival.
